Privacy Policy

Shiftchi - Shift Management Application

Last Updated: February 2026

Summary: Shiftchi is a shift management application that takes your privacy seriously. We only collect information necessary for the app's functionality and never share it with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Phone Number: For registration and authentication
  • Google Email (Optional): Only if you sign in with Google

1.2 Shift Information

  • Date and time of your work shifts
  • Work location (optional)
  • Your personal notes
  • Break and overtime information

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Error logs (only for troubleshooting)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • Shift Management: Store and display your work shifts
  • Google Calendar Sync: Add shifts to your Google Calendar (only if enabled)
  • Reminders: Send notifications for upcoming shifts
  • Backup: Securely store your information
  • App Improvement: Fix bugs and optimize performance

3. Google Calendar Integration

Important: Using Google Calendar is completely optional.

If you choose to sync your shifts with Google Calendar:

  • We only create, edit, or delete events related to your work shifts
  • No other events from your calendar are read or modified
  • You can disable this feature at any time
  • Disconnecting revokes all access immediately

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All information is encrypted using high security standards
  • Secure Servers: We use Supabase service with international security standards
  • Limited Access: Only you have access to your information
  • Backups: Your backups are stored encrypted

5. Information Sharing

We never sell or rent your information to third parties.

Your information is only shared in the following cases:

  • Google Calendar: Only if enabled by you
  • Legal Requirements: If requested by judicial authorities

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all stored information
  • Edit: Change or correct your information
  • Delete: Completely delete your account and all information
  • Export: Receive a backup copy of your information
  • Disconnect: Revoke Google Calendar access at any time

7. Data Deletion

To completely delete your information:

  • Go to app settings
  • Select "Delete Account"
  • All your information will be permanently deleted

8. Children

This application is not designed for individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Important changes will be notified through the application.

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy:
