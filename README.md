## English Version

### 📱 About the App

Shiftchi is a smart shift management application that helps users easily manage their work shifts and sync with Google Calendar.

### 🔗 Important Links

- [Home Page](https://fuladpanje.github.io/Shiftchi/)
- [Privacy Policy](https://fuladpanje.github.io/Shiftchi/privacy-policy)
- [Terms of Service](https://fuladpanje.github.io/Shiftchi/terms-of-service)

### ✨ Features

- 📊 Shift Management
- 🔔 Smart Reminders
- 📅 Google Calendar Sync
- 📈 Reports and Statistics
- 💾 Secure Backup
- 🌙 Day/Night Mode

### 🔒 Privacy

We take user privacy very seriously:
- Your information is stored encrypted
- We never share your information
- Google Calendar sync is completely optional
- You can delete your account at any time
